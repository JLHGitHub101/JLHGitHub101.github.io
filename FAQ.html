FAQ

Why are we Hiring?

We are a new company and looking to expend. We are looking to grow the truck company into more just a basic trunking company that moves loads, but we also want to build a brand.

Why do we give paid training?

As a new learner, you will learn how to drive the “dhanoa” way which fits best for our company and for the drivers’ safety. Even with minimum experience, we want you to get the best out of it, all the profits and benefits that we provide.

How is it working in a team?

Some drivers prefer long hual loads and doing so in a team. Teams consist of two drivers ususally long hauling from east to west coast. or vice verse. When one driver finishes thier logbook the other driver takes the load and completes the run.
